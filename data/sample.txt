The **University of Ilorin Teaching Hospital (UITH)** is a foremost tertiary healthcare institution in Nigeria, affiliated with the **University of Ilorin (UNILORIN)**. It serves as a **training, research, and referral center** for medical students, doctors, and other healthcare professionals. Located in **Ilorin, Kwara State**, UITH provides specialized medical services to patients across **North-Central Nigeria** and beyond.

## **6. Challenges Facing UITH**  
Despite its successes, UITH faces some challenges, including:  
- **Inadequate funding** leading to delays in equipment upgrades.  
- **Overcrowding** due to high patient influx from neighboring states.  
- **Shortage of specialized medical personnel** (e.g., neurosurgeons, oncologists).  
- **Infrastructure limitations**, including power supply issues.
